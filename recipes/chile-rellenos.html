Chile Rellenos Recipe

Authentic chile relleno recipe handed down for generations in my family. A fried Anaheim chile pepper stuffed with cheese.

Ingredients

  • 6 Anaheim Chile Peppers
  • 1 Package Queso Asadero
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 3/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
    • <1i>Cup Vegetable Shortening

Directions

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Preheat oven and line baking sheet with aluminum
  3. Place peppers onto baking sheet and into oven for 10 minutes and let them cool in covered bowl for 15 minutes
  4. Skin peppers and cut a slit into them, removing seeds and core
  5. Rinse peppers
  6. Mix egg yolks and baking powder
  7. Gently fold beaten egg whites into mixture
  8. Melt vegetable shortening in skillet and roll stuffed peppers in flour, dip into egg mixture, and ley them into the shortening for about 5 minutes per side
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
